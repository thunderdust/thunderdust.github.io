I was born in a remote area in Sichuan, China called Panzhihua. It is a city famous for its steel production, and the used-to-be Aisa's biggest hydroelectric power station. I spend first 6 years of my life there. I have developed my love for nature since then.

After that I moved to Chengdu with my mother. It is a cozy and historical city yet with all the modern fashions. I grew up there and became an enthusiast for Ancient Chinese Literatures. Around my 11, I fell in love with basketball and now it has become a life-long interest for me. When I was a bit older than then, I also found myself fund of poem, hip-hop and street dance.

My love for software engineering and programming does not come to me until I started my university life in Singapore. I choosed Computer Science as my major, and learned a lot during the precious 4 years.

Now I have finished my bachelor degree. I have been working in JAC Recruitment since July 2015. While working hard, I am still learning new stuff and hopefully I will have a more diverse and remarkable career in future.
