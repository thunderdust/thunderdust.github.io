Sticky footer with auto fading

This is a demo page for [Sticky footer with auto fading]

This footer always stick at bottom of current page, and automatically hide itself unless:

1) The page is short enough to be entirely displayed;

OR

2) The user scrolls to the bottom of the page.

You can scroll this page to try out, enjoy :)
